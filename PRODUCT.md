# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Businesses, startups, and enterprises seeking a high-quality, reliable web and mobile app development partner in India/globally. They value technical expertise, modern aesthetics, seamless performance, and clear business-aligned engineering.

## Product Purpose
The Kraftostech website redesign aims to establish the company as a premium, cutting-edge development agency. The new React-based website will showcase their mobile (Flutter, iOS, Android) and web (React, Laravel, Next.js) capabilities, build credibility through testimonials/case studies, and drive conversions with a polished, low-friction contact flow.

## Brand Personality
- **Voice**: Precise, tech-forward, professional, collaborative.
- **Tone**: Modern, sleek, innovative, reliable, premium yet approachable.
- **Color Palette**:
  - Primary Blue: `#0F3A7D` (Trust, tech, credibility)
  - Accent Cyan: `#00D9FF` (Electric cyan for innovation, energy)
  - Neutral Gray: `#F5F5F5` (Clean breathing space)
  - Dark Slate: `#1A1A1A` (Sophisticated deep slate for text and layout depth)

## Anti-references
- Cluttered layouts or generic startup landing templates.
- Over-saturated, generic "AI-purple" neon gradients.
- Fake dashboard screenshots or generic stock illustrations.
- Jittery, unmotivated, or distracting micro-animations.

## Design Principles
1. **Geometric, Modern Minimalist Layout**: Rely on asymmetric spacing, a clean 8px grid system, and bold headlines with tight letter spacing for a premium feel.
2. **Motivated, Smooth Animations**: Staggered scroll reveals, hover lifts with tinted drop-shadows, and smooth page transitions using GSAP and Framer Motion.
3. **High Breathing Space & Clarity**: Keep content concise (headlines under 8 words, subtext under 20 words) with plenty of negative space to direct attention to CTAs.
4. **Contrast & Accessibility-First**: Ensure all text elements meet WCAG AA contrast ratios against their respective backgrounds, with full support for `prefers-reduced-motion`.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratio ≥ 4.5:1 for body copy.
- Full respect for user reduced motion media queries.
- Clean semantic HTML layouts (nav, main, section, footer).
